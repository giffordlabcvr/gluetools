HCV-GLUE

Analysis of Hepatitis C Virus for Biologically-relevant Sequence Motifs

HCV-GLUE is a public web tool providing automated analysis of HCV sequence data. Clinicians and researchers can submit nucleotide virus sequences to the tool. Each sequence is analysed within the context of the known HCV genotypes and subtypes. An interactive web report is generated, showing in detail the biologically-relevant sequence motifs.

Expert Knowledge

The analysis tool is based on the GLUE software framework. For the GLUE Hepatitis C Virus project, members of the HCV research community have come together to provide the detailed and up-to-date expert knowledge of the virus which is necessary to perform the automated analysis.
