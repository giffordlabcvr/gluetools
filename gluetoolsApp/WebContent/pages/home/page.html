GLUE is a data-centric software package for capturing and developing nucleotide-level evolutionary hypotheses for virus and endogenous viral element (EVE) genomes.

It enables the dissemination of these hypotheses on the web and their utilisation in wider software systems.

Read the Overview to understand the scope of GLUE and whether it is the right tool for your purposes.
